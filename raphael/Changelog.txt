2020-04-15 update changelog

## ROM and Device-specific sources
* Latest source sync (April Security Patch)
* Update graphics, gps, and media libs to CAF LA.UM.8.1.r1-14500-sm8150.0
* Auto-brightness Improvement
* Force-encryption enabled
* Update ANX Camera to v175
* Other miscellaneous improvements and cleanups

## Chaldea Kernel [reborn]
* Full-rebase over CAF Platform Tag LA.UM.8.1.r1-14700-sm8150.0 (yes, finally not an iMMENSITY-based kernel anymore :D)
  With Google ACK android-4.14-q branch and Linux stable v4.14.176 merged.
* Compiled with Avalon Clang 11.0.1 + RELR Relocation and LLVM Polly optimization enabled inline
* Pure PELT patches and several scheduler optimization merged
* (Still) Mi9 FOD
* Refine DC Dimming tuning (lower brightness intensity allowed)
* and many other optimizations and fixes added for stability

## Notes
* Custom vendor variant, clean flash is a MUST if you comes from non-custom vendor.
* Gapps is included, no vanilla version.
